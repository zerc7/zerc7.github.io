Δx→Δy→Δz\Delta x\to\Delta y\to \Delta z Δx→Δy→Δz

dzdx=dzdydydx\frac{\text d z}{\text d x} = \frac{\text d z}{\text d y}\frac{\text d y}{\text d x} dxdz​=dydz​dxdy​

Case 2:

x=g(s),y=h(s),z=k(x,y)x=g(s), y=h(s), z=k(x,y) x=g(s),y=h(s),z=k(x,y)

Δs→ΔxΔy→Δz\Delta s\to \begin{matrix} \Delta x\\\\ \Delta y \end{matrix} \to \Delta z Δs→ΔxΔy​→Δz

dzds=∂z∂xdxds+∂z∂ydyds\frac{\text d z}{\text d s} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\frac{\text d x}{\text d s} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\frac{\text d y}{\text d s} dsdz​=∂x∂z​dsdx​+∂y∂z​dsdy​

L(θ)=∑n=1NCn(θ)→∂L(θ)∂w=∑n=1N∂Cn(θ)∂wL(\theta)=\sum_{n=1}^{N}C^n(\theta)\to \frac{\partial L(\theta)}{\partial w}=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\frac{\partial C^n(\theta)}{\partial w} L(θ)=n=1∑N​Cn(θ)→∂w∂L(θ)​=n=1∑N​∂w∂Cn(θ)​
